In my opinion, the best class in the game are Warlocks. (see picture above)
They offer a wide range of different builds that can be tailored into certain aspects
of the game. Currently my favorite build is an arc build that focuses heavily on melee
and ability regeneration. When done right, it allows for near constant melee ability
uptime.

When compared to the other two classes in the game, Warlocks are considered to be the
ones treated more favorably by the developers. A couple years ago, DeeJ, one of the
community managers at the time at Bungie, was known for his bias toward Warlocks.
At almost every reveal event or livestream bungie would put on, he was always donning
his Dead Orbit (this is one of the 3 main factions within the game) geared Warlock.
Sadly, he left bungie to pursue a new career at another game studio.
